7. **Technologies**: List all of the technologies you used to complete the project, to show your proficiency with these tools.

## **1. Project Overview**
This project analyzes historical sales data to identify trends, predict future performance, and provide actionable insights for optimizing inventory and marketing strategies.

## **2. Problem Statement**
The company experiences inconsistent sales growth and wants to understand which products drive revenue, how seasonal trends impact performance, and whether marketing efforts are effective.

## **3. Data Sources**
- **Sales_2023.csv** (1.2GB, 1.5M rows) from internal CRM system.

## **4. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing**
- Filled missing values with median for numeric columns.
- Removed outliers using IQR method.
- One-hot encoded categorical variables.

## **5. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
EDA revealed that 60% of revenue comes from 5 products, with a clear seasonal spike in December.

## **6. Analysis & Insights**
1. Product A is the highest contributor to revenue but has declining sales.
2. Seasonal promotions drive significant sales spikes.
3. Marketing spend has a positive ROI but could be optimized.

## **8. Modeling**
Used a Random Forest Regressor with R² Score: 0.85.

## **9. Conclusion & Recommendations**
1. Launch a targeted promotion for Product A.
2. Allocate more budget to Q4 campaigns.
